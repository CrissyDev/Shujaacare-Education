About ShujaaCare

ShujaaCare is an innovative digital platform designed to make cervical cancer education accessible, engaging, and rewarding. We believe that informed women are empowered women. By combining interactive learning modules with a token-based rewards system, we make health education both impactful and incentivizing for women across Africa.
